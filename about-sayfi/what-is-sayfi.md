# What is SayFi?

**SayFi** is a new-generation **AI-native perpetual futures exchange**. Trading does not need to start with dense charts and complex panels. You can express intent through **natural conversation** — with voice support where available — and let your **AI trading agent** turn that intent into executable orders and position actions. Final execution still depends on your **on-chain authorization and confirmation**.

***

### Core concept

> **「Say it, Trade it, Shield it.」**\
> Perpetual trading on traditional DEXs and CEXs often means switching between leverage settings, TP/SL controls, and multiple screens. SayFi pulls that flow into a chat box. **Trading becomes a conversation, not a wall of buttons**. You describe your direction and preferences. Your agent helps complete the setup in one flow.

**You can place trades in chat. You can also add optional Zero Loss Insurance or Double Profit Insurance for liquidation-related protection or extra upside after profit targets are reached**. For details, see [Insurance](../insurance/).

***

### What you get in SayFi

#### Your private AI trading agent 💎

Each user gets a dedicated **AI Trader**. Chat takes you from “here is what I want to do” to placing orders, adjusting positions, and checking exposure. You can create **multiple Traders**, each with its own sub-account. **Funds and strategies stay isolated**. You can create up to **10**. This works well for different strategies and risk profiles.

The assistant uses **market data, information signals, and your historical behavior** to suggest entry ideas, add-on plans, and option comparisons. **You stay in control at every step**. Nothing is executed unless you explicitly instruct or confirm it. The assistant does not trade automatically on your behalf.

#### Trade by talking

SayFi supports **text** input. It also supports **voice** input where the client allows it. You do not need to memorize every order type first. You can describe direction, rough margin size, and TP/SL intent in plain language, and the assistant helps convert that into concrete actions.

#### USDC perpetuals across multiple asset classes

Beyond major crypto assets, SayFi also supports **USDC-settled perpetuals** across precious metals, energy, FX, indices, and commodities. Supported markets depend on the live platform listing. You can ask the AI assistant which symbols are currently available.

#### Insurance features 💎

Protect capital. Expand upside.

* **Zero Loss Insurance**: for covered positions, **if liquidation happens, the product pays the full initial margin for that position according to the policy terms**. In effect, the full margin is restored. **It is currently available for 20 major perpetual pairs including BTC and ETH, plus commodities and FX**. Coverage terms are **24h / 72h / 168h (7 days)**.
* **Double Profit Insurance**: once floating PnL **reaches the target**, the product adds **an equal amount of extra floating profit**. This is often described as **100% → 200%**. Premiums are **priced in real time** based on market conditions, and **cancellation with refund follows product rules**. It is also available for **20 major perpetual pairs including BTC and ETH, plus commodities and FX**, with terms of **24h / 72h / 168h (7 days)**.

Premiums, trigger conditions, and refund rules **always follow the confirmation screen and official documentation**. Do not rely on unofficial claims from external channels.

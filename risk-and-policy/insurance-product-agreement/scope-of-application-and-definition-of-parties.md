# Scope of Application and Definition of Parties

### Scope

The **trading protection products** covered by this agreement set mainly include:

* **Zero Loss Insurance**: attaches to an **isolated** position. If the agreed liquidation-prevention path is triggered, the product pays according to the policy terms. Current availability depends on deployed markets such as BTC and ETH.
* **Double Profit Insurance**: pays extra benefits under agreed conditions such as reaching the floating-profit threshold. Supported markets and durations also depend on the live app configuration.

These products are **not** life insurance, health insurance, or traditional property insurance. Premiums, durations such as `24h`, `72h`, and `168h`, and supported markets always follow the purchase confirmation screen and the [Products](../../insurance/products.md) page.

***

### Parties

| Role                             | Description                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **User / policyholder**          | A natural person or legally authorized entity that connects a wallet, accepts the required agreement version, and completes payment in the app.                                                     |
| **Platform / protocol operator** | The party that provides perpetual trading and optional insurance features. The formal legal entity name, registration details, and contact information follow the app and official legal documents. |

***

### Agreement version

Before a new purchase, renewal, or an **open-and-insure** action, the system can require the user to accept the **current insurance agreement version**, such as `required_agreement_version`. If the required version is not accepted, underwriting should not complete.

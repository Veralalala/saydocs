# Risk & Policy

This section covers the rules, disclosures, and operating boundaries that matter before you trade or buy insurance products on SayFi.

Use it to understand what you are responsible for, what the product covers, and which scenarios remain outside coverage.

***

These pages focus on wallet safety and transaction verification.

Read them before you sign messages or approve on-chain actions:

* [**Private Key and Mnemonic Phrase Management Tips**](user-operation/private-key-and-mnemonic-phrase-management-tips.md)

These pages explain scenarios where losses may still occur and where insurance does not apply:

* [**List of scenarios not covered by insurance**](risk-disclosure/list-of-scenarios-not-covered-by-insurance.md)

***

### How to use this section

Read **User operation** first if you are new.

Read **Insurance Product Agreement** before buying or renewing insurance.

Read **Risk disclosure** before relying on coverage assumptions in volatile markets.

***

### Core principles

* You remain responsible for wallet security, signatures, and transaction confirmation.
* Product terms, coverage scope, refunds, and exclusions follow the live app and official agreement pages.
* External claims never override the confirmation screen, risk controls, or legal terms in this section.

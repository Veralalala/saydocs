# User operation

This section covers the user actions that most often lead to preventable loss.

Read these pages before you connect a wallet, sign messages, or approve on-chain requests.

***

### What this section covers

#### Wallet secret management

Your private key and mnemonic control your assets.

Use this page to review storage, backup, device hygiene, and loss-response basics:

* [**Private Key and Mnemonic Phrase Management Tips**](private-key-and-mnemonic-phrase-management-tips.md)

#### Signature verification and anti-phishing

Most sensitive actions rely on wallet signatures.

Use this page to check domains, contract targets, EIP-712 fields, and replay-risk details before you sign:

* [**Signature Request Verification Checklist (Anti-Phishing)**](signature-request-verification-checklist-anti-phishing.md)

***

### Core rules

* Never share your private key or mnemonic.
* Never approve a request you do not fully understand.
* Treat urgency, fear, and reward bait as phishing signals.

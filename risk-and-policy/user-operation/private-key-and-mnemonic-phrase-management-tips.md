# Private Key and Mnemonic Phrase Management Tips

### Core principle

**Whoever controls the private key or mnemonic controls the assets at that address.** SayFi never asks for your private key or mnemonic. Treat any request to "verify your account," "unfreeze funds," or "claim compensation" as a scam.

***

### Recommended practices

1. **Back up your mnemonic offline**\
   Store it on paper or hardware media. Do not rely only on photos, cloud drives, chat apps, or email.
2. **Separate "connect" from "sign"**\
   Connecting a wallet starts a session. Sensitive actions can still require approval in your wallet popup. Do not approve requests in bulk without reading them.
3. **Use one trusted device for your main wallet**\
   Do not import your main wallet on jailbroken, rooted, or untrusted public devices. Keep test wallets separate from primary funds.
4. **If a device is lost**\
   If mnemonic exposure risk increases, move funds to a new address as soon as possible in a safe environment. Retire the old mnemonic and main account if your wallet supports it.

***

### What SayFi can and cannot do

* The AI trading assistant **does not** and **cannot** export or store your private key.
* There is **no** recovery flow where SayFi restores a lost mnemonic.
* If you lose it without a backup, recovery is usually **not possible**.
